# **App Name**: FinancioAI

## Core Features:

- Invoice Generation & Management: Create, edit, send, and track invoices with customizable data fields (bank details, company logo). Generates invoices in PDF format for email or WhatsApp sharing.
- Customer Management: Maintain a customer database with contact details and payment history.
- Income & Expense Tracking: Record and categorize income and expenses to track cash flow. Records may be categorized into day, month and year.
- Financial Reporting: Generate visual reports and statistics of income, expenses, and overall financial performance over time. Generate and analyze by day, month, or year.
- AI-Powered Expense Categorization: An AI tool analyzes descriptions of transactions to automatically categorize income and expenses into relevant categories. LLM may elect to use information from previous categorizations.
- Simple Authentication: Provide a secure and easy way for users to log in to their accounts.

## Style Guidelines:

- Background color: Dark gray (#121212) to create an elegant and modern feel, in line with Apple's design aesthetics.
- Primary color: Electric blue (#7DF9FF) to provide a sophisticated contrast to the dark background and ensure elements stand out.
- Accent color: Sky blue (#87CEEB) as an analogous contrast that is of a much lighter brightness for secondary interactive elements and highlights, offering a seamless and uncluttered UI.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for headlines, providing a techy, modern, and stylish look.
- Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for body text, for maximum legibility.
- Use simple, clean, and professional icons for easy navigation.
- Maintain a minimalist and uncluttered layout.
